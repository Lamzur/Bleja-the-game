Bleja - the game
Artwork Story Our team Contact us!

A 2D game taking place in our hometown, capturing the essence of times spent there. The name, which is a slang for get together is something we used quite often, therefore it became a symbol for any form of social encounters between us. Taking inspiration from games such as Stardew Valley, and Streets of Rogue, it is mainly a fighting game with exploring, NPCs, mini games and more! We strive towards creating a game that is not only interesting to play, but visually stunning, along with music that everyone will enjoy. Follow us, and you will see the creative process behind the idea, as well as everything else you want to know about our journey.
